Analysis 1

Conference Growth Over Time

Presentation counts by year, showing the trajectory of ARNOVA conference participation from 2012 to 2025.

Key Observations

  • Peak participation in 2018 (611 presentations) at the Austin, TX conference.
  • COVID-19 impact is visible in 2020–2022, with virtual and post-pandemic conferences showing 50–60% reduction from peak.
  • Recovery trend beginning in 2023, with 2025 returning to 371 presentations.
  • Pre-pandemic growth from 2015–2019 showed consistent upward trajectory.
